Clients Question

we understand the importance of clear communication. If you have any questions or concerns, our dedicated team is here to assist you

What safety measures are in place for younger players?

Certified coaches, mandatory gear, and strict guidelines.

What age groups can join the hockey club?

All ages are welcome, from youth to adult levels.

How often are training sessions held?

Weekly sessions, lasting 1.5 to 2 hours each.

Do you provide gear for beginners?

We offer guidance and may provide initial gear.

Are there competitive play opportunities?

Yes, at various skill levels.

How can parents get involved in the community?

Volunteer as coaches, assist with events, or join the booster club.
